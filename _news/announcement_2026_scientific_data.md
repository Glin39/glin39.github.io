Our dataset paper [*A longitudinal dataset of physiological, hormonal, metabolic, and self-reported menstrual health data*](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41597-026-06805-3) has been published in [*Nature Scientific Data*](https://www.nature.com/sdata/)! This is one of the first comprehensive multimodal menstrual health dataset including wearable, hormonal, and metabolic data.
